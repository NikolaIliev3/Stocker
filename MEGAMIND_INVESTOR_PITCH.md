## 🔬 TECHNOLOGY DEEP DIVE

### Machine Learning Architecture

**Ensemble Models**: Combines multiple algorithms for robustness
- RandomForest (100 trees, depth-limited to prevent overfitting)
- LightGBM/XGBoost (gradient boosting with regularization)
- Logistic Regression (linear baseline)
- VotingClassifier (weighted ensemble)
- StackingClassifier (meta-learner for optimal combination)

**Feature Engineering**: 130+ features automatically selected and optimized
- Technical Indicators: RSI, MACD, EMAs, Bollinger Bands, ATR, Stochastic, ADX
- Price Action: Momentum, volatility, volume patterns, support/resistance
- Fundamental Metrics: P/E, P/B, debt ratios, growth rates, profitability
- Enhanced Features: Market microstructure, news sentiment, sector strength
- Feature Interactions: Polynomial features for complex pattern detection

**Class Balancing**: SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Oversampling) prevents bias
- Automatically detects class imbalance (e.g., 60% UP vs 40% DOWN)
- Creates synthetic samples for minority class
- Maintains model fairness and accuracy

**Market Regime Detection**: Separate models for different market conditions
- Bull Market Model: Optimized for uptrend predictions
- Bear Market Model: Optimized for downtrend predictions
- Sideways Market Model: Optimized for range-bound trading

### Continuous Learning Mechanisms

1. **Prediction Verification Loop**
   - Every prediction automatically verified after target date
   - Records actual vs predicted outcomes
   - Calculates accuracy and confidence calibration
   - Feeds results back to training pipeline

2. **Backtesting to Training Pipeline**
   - Converts backtest results to training samples
   - Automatically retrains models when accuracy drops
   - Adapts to changing market conditions

3. **Trend Change Learning**
   - Tracks day accuracy (predicted days vs actual days)
   - Tracks price accuracy (predicted prices vs actual prices)
   - Adjusts multipliers based on historical performance
   - Smooth adjustments prevent overcorrection

4. **Weight Optimization**
   - Analyzes failure patterns from backtests
   - Adjusts rule-based indicator weights
   - Reduces weight on overconfident predictions
   - Improves ensemble balance
